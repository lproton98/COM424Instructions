Important Safety Instructions

  • DO NOT attempt to repair or adjust any electrical or mechanical functions on this unit.
  • DO NOT plug in the cord with wet hands: electric shock could result.
  • DO NOT block air inlet or outlet
  • NEVER operate the unit without water in the tank.

Safety Notes

  • If moisture forms on walls or windows of room, turn off humidifier
  • This is an electrical appliance and requires attention when in use. Humidifier should be unplugged when not in use.
