Humidifier Instructions

Read all instructions before use

  • Choose an INDOOR location for your humidifier (DO NOT use outdoors)
  • Locate your humidifier on an inside wall near an electrical outlet (AVOID places near heat sources).
  • Place your humidifier on a firm, flat, level surface. A waterproof mat or pad is recommended for use under the humidifier. (DO NOT place on a carpet or rug)
  • Plug the appliance directly into a 120V AC electrical outlet
  • Place the humidifier in an area away from children (DO NOT allow children near the unit).
  • Face the Moisture Outlet away from the wall (facing it towards a wall may cause moisture damage).
  • DO NOT place the humidifier under overhanging surfaces
  • DO NOT use humidifier in an area where there is 50% or more humidity
  • NEVER place housing under water flow or immerse in liquids.
